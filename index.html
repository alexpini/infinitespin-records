services

PRODUCING

Infinitespin Records is a full service recording studio that offers guidance at all stages of the music making process. Whether it is songwriting, developing an artist, or finding a vision for the overall production, we are here to help. Our vast network of musicians and producers enables us to assist from the ground level.

TRACKING

Our unique vintage consoles, analog outboard gear, tape machines, and cutting edge Protools HD system, in addition to a classically built live room/control room, gives our clients the opportunity to work in an environment where quality of sound is not in question. The goal becomes discovering the sounds you want.

MIXING

The control room at Infinitepsin Records stands in a league of its own. Classically built with a 28 ft x 4ft deep bass trap in the back of the room, acoustics throughout the ceiling, and two walls full of sand, the room reveals all the sonic elements of your project.
