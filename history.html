HISTORY

A BRIEF HISTORY

Infinitespin Records is a uniquely built studio from the 80s, utilizing classic acoustic technology alongside unique analog equipment and cutting edge digital gear. The studio carries a reputation in and of itself: having been a drummers studio for many years (Sheila E. and Simon Phillips), the studio is a unique facility where sculpting your desired sounds becomes a fun process.

  • Past Clients:
    Toto, Alan Parsons, Rick Braun, Billy Ray Cyrus, Virgil Donati, Roberta Flack, Marcelo Gaggliotti, Paul Gilbert, Steve Hackett, Hiromi, James Horner, Al Jarreau, Nik Kershaw, Dave Kerzner, Bob Kulick, Limp Bizkit, Chris Marr, Tony McAlpine, Jay Oliver, David Pack, John Payne, Planet X, Simon Phillips, Mike Portnoy, Billie Rainbird, Joe Satriani, Philippe Saisse, Derek Sherinian, Michael Schenker, Billy Sheehan, Chris Squier, Carl Verheyen, Michael Voss, Milen Vrabevsky, Dave Weckl, Kirk Whalum, Peter White, Lyle Workman
  • Past Music Production:
    Blue Notes Records, Capitol Records, Heaven Productions Music, Sony Disco, Sony Music, Ace Bandage, Sheila E., Paul Jackson Jr., Patti LaBelle, LeAnne Rimes
  • Past Film & TV Production:
    20th Century Fox Films, Blind Mule Productions, Fox Sports, Miramax, Spirit Dance Entertainment, Wayne Brady, Wes Craven
