Dave Mason

    David Mason (born 10 May 1946) is an English singer-songwriter and guitarist from Worcester, who first found fame with the rock band Traffic. Over the course of his career, Mason has played with many notable pop and rock musicians, including Paul McCartney, George Harrison, The Rolling Stones, Jimi Hendrix, Eric Clapton, Michael Jackson, and Fleetwood Mac. In 2004, Mason was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
